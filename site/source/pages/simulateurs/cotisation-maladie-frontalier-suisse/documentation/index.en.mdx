As a **cross-border worker in Switzerland** covered by the French health insurance, your contribution is calculated each year by Urssaf based on your income.

## The formula

> **Annual contribution = (base − allowance) × {tauxCotisation}%**

- **Income base**: the sum of your **salaries** and your **other income** for the year.
- **Allowance**: **{tauxAbattement}% of the annual social security ceiling (PASS)**, revalued each year.
- **Rate**: **{tauxCotisation}%**.

The contribution estimated from this income will apply two years later.

## Partial years (prorating)

If your affiliation starts or ends during the year, that year’s contribution is prorated to the number of days of affiliation:

> **Contribution for the year = annual contribution × days of affiliation ÷ number of days in the year**
